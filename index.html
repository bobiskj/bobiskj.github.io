Dead cells

Dead Cells is a side-scrollinger game developed and published by Mundfish. The game follows the journey of a red-colored cell who is on a mission to uncover the truth about his past and the world around him. The game features fast-paced combat, challenging levels, and various weapons and abilities that the player can unlock as they progress through the game. The game is available for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, PC, and iOS and Android devices.

The Rusty Sword

The Rusty Sword is the first weapon that appears in the player's possession. It is the starting weapon until the player purchases the Random Melee Weapon upgrade from the Collector. But even after this, it can be obtained from a secret cache at the beginning of the game.
